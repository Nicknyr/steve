Hunter College Web Development Club

Our Club

The Hunter College Web Development Club founded in the Fall of 2015 is always looking for new members. The purpose of our club is for students interested in learning web development to come together and learn together. Our meetings are currently held every Wednesday at 1pm.


Our club is currently looking to volunteer our time for non-profits and charities that are in need of a new website. If you're a non-profit and interested in a pro-bono website please get in touch

  • Workshops

    The primary purpose of our club is to collaborate and learn web development. Our club does this by members regularly teaching workshops on web related technologies

  • On Campus

    Our club regularly hosts on campus events open to all Hunter students. Any student interested is encouraged to attend and our events are appropriate for any skill level

  • Off Campus

    The club also explores off campus get togethers and meetups throughout NYC.Seminars, meetup groups, and hackathons etc...
